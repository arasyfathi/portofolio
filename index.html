About Me

Engineering smart systems that make a real impact.

I’m an Electrical Engineering student who enjoys building physical and digital systems that genuinely solve real-world problems. Most of my work sits at the intersection of the Internet of Things (IoT), hardware automation, and artificial intelligence.

I like taking ideas from the first concept all the way to a functional prototype — whether that means designing precise circuit logic, programming microcontrollers, or structuring reliable real-time web dashboards.

In past projects, I’ve trained YOLOv8 models for automated waste classification, built real-time IoT dashboards using Blynk.Console and Blynk.cloud, and developed environmental monitoring systems using ESP32 and Flask. My goal is always the same: smart, scalable, and efficient engineering solutions.
